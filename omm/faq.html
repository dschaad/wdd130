FAQs

Why Am I Doing This?

I hope this site will help people develop a general idea of Minecraft mods and have a more enjoyable experience playing with mods that fit their playstyle. I hope to appeal to the people who are simply looking for a general overview of a mod.

What Is My Experience with Minecraft Mods?

I have a lot of experience with using mods, especially in Minecraft. While there are guides and tutorials for specific mods online, I always felt these overviews were either far too drawn out or unnecessarily overexplained. I felt I had to watch hours of explanations in order to decide whether or not I'd enjoy a mod, which didn't seem ideal.

What Do I Need To Know?

The only information necessary for those using my site will be a general understanding of Minecraft as a game. Also an understanding of mods as tools to change the behavior or content of video games.
